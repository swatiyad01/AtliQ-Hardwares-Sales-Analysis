# AtliQ Hardwares Sales Analysis

### 🚀 Project Overview

Analyzed sales data of **AtliQ Hardwares** using Excel and Power Query to build an end-to-end sales analytics solution, transforming raw data into actionable business insights

The project covers the complete workflow:

**Data Extraction → Transformation → Modeling → Reporting → Business Insights**

### 🎯 Business Problem

- Understand sales growth trends
- Identify top & bottom performing products
- Analyze customer contribution
- Evaluate market performance vs targets

### 📌 Key Insights

- 📈 **Significant Business Growth**
    - Sales grew from **$196.7M (2020) to $598.9M (2021)** (~204% increase)
- 🖥️ **Top Performing Products**
    - Products like *AQ Electron 4 3600* and *AQ Smash 2* showed exceptional growth (500%+ YoY)
- 📉 **Product Performance Analysis**
    - Identified **Top 5 and Bottom 5 products** based on quantity sold
- 🌍 **Market Insights**
    - **India** contributed the highest revenue with **$161.3M in 2021**
- 🧑‍💼 **Customer Performance**
    - Customers like **Amazon, AtliQ Exclusive, Flipkart** showed strong growth (200%–400%)
- 🆕 **New Product Impact**
    - Newly launched products generated **$176.2M revenue in 2021**

### 🛠️ **Tools & Techniques**

**Tools/Used**

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query for data cleaning & Transformation
- Pivot table for Data Summarization
- Power pivot for Data Modelling
- DAX measures for calculated columns

### 💡 Business Recommendation

- Scale **top-performing products**
- Optimize or remove **low-performing SKUs**
- Focus on **high-growth customers**
- Expand in **high-performing markets (India)**
- Invest in **new product innovation**

### 🔗 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Sales Analytics can:
✔ Identify growth drivers

✔ Optimize product strategy

✔ Enable data-driven decisions
